# Privacy Policy for AtmosNet

**Effective Date:** March 25, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 25, 2026

AtmosNet Technologies Ltd. ("we," "us," or "our") operates the AtmosNet mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Service.

By using AtmosNet, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Service.

---

## What We Collect

### From Your Device

When you opt in to contribute weather data, we collect:

- **Barometric pressure readings** from your device's pressure sensor
- **GPS location**, rounded to the nearest 500-meter grid before transmission
- **Timestamp** of the observation
- **Device motion data** (to detect if device is stationary or moving)
- **A hashed identifier** derived from your device (not your actual device ID)

### From Your Account

- **AtmosPoints balance** and transaction history
- **Daily contribution streak** (for gamification purposes)
- **Opt-in preferences** for data collection

### We Do NOT Collect

- Your exact GPS coordinates (always rounded to 500m grid)
- Your real name, email address, or phone number (unless you provide it separately)
- Your device's advertising ID or IMEI
- Photos, contacts, or other personal data
- Data when you have not explicitly opted in

---

## How We Use Your Data

1. **Weather Analysis:** We combine your pressure reading with other nearby observations to create hyperlocal weather models.

2. **Quality Validation:** We compare readings against public weather APIs to ensure accuracy.

3. **Rewards:** We award AtmosPoints for validated observations to incentivize contributions.

4. **Service Improvement:** We analyze aggregated patterns to improve our models and algorithms.

---

## Data Protection Measures

### On Your Device

Before any data leaves your phone:

- **GPS coordinates are rounded** to the nearest 500-meter grid cell
- **Device ID is hashed** using SHA-256 with a salt unique to our system
- **Data is batched** and uploaded only over WiFi (by default) to minimize impact

### On Our Servers

- **Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted via TLS 1.3
- **Encryption at rest:** All stored data is encrypted
- **Access controls:** Strict role-based access to production systems
- **Retention limits:** Raw observations retained for 90 days; aggregated data retained indefinitely
- **No third-party analytics:** We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tracking

---

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data

Your individual observations are never sold, rented, or traded.

### Enterprise API Data

We sell access to **aggregated, anonymized weather data** to enterprise customers. This data:
- Is averaged across thousands of observations
- Cannot be traced back to any individual
- Is location-aggregated to grid cells (500m or larger)
- Contains no device identifiers or timestamps that could enable re-identification

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as:
- In response to a subpoena or court order
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing

## Your Rights and Choices

### Opt-Out

You can stop contributing data at any time:
- Toggle "Data Contribution" to OFF in app Settings
- Uninstall the app

Existing observations already transmitted will not be deleted (they are anonymized).

### Data Deletion

To request deletion of your AtmosPoints account and associated data:
- Email: privacy@atmosnet.io
- Include your account identifier (found in Settings > Account)

We will process your request within 30 days. Note that anonymized weather observations cannot be deleted as they contain no identifying information.

### Access and Portability

You can export your AtmosPoints transaction history from the app (Settings > Data Export).

## AtmosPoints System

AtmosPoints are **not a cryptocurrency**. They:
- Have no cash value and cannot be redeemed for fiat currency
- Cannot be transferred, sold, or traded outside the AtmosNet ecosystem
- Can only be used for premium app features, partner data products, or rewards within the AtmosNet marketplace
- May expire if an account is inactive for 12 consecutive months
